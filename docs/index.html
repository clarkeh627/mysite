About me

I’m a Ph.D. student in physics at Stanford University. I’m interested in learning about dark matter, one of the most intriguing and elusive mysteries of our universe today. I have worked on three different experiments that attempt to detect in here on Earth – the NEWS-G experiment, which uses spherical proportional counters, the PICO experiment, using bubble chambers, and now the LZ experiment, a liquid xenon time projection chamber.
